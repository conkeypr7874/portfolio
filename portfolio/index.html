About me!

Hello, my name is Paris Conkey! I am currently a third year student at the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire, majoring in Graphic Communications. A couple of my biggest accomplishement are graduating high school as Summa Cum Laude with a 4.0 GPA, and continuing my achievements to college with a 3.92 so far and winning the national championships for the UWEC Club Volleyball team in 2022. I have been an athlete my whole life and played volleyball for 10 years before having to put more of my focus into my college career. When I’m not working or busy with school, I love spending my free time with my family and friends, playing with my cat Lola, working out, snuggled in bed with a good book or watching a show. I have a passion for creating and can’t wait to see where my design career takes me to continue persuing just that.
